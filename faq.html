General Questions

What is EMERSON?

EMERSON is a community-driven meme cryptocurrency inspired by internet culture and designed to bring fun, engagement, and potential value to its holders. It combines the playful aspects of meme coins with a solid foundation of security and community governance.

When was EMERSON launched?

EMERSON was launched in June 2024 with a fair launch distribution model, ensuring equal opportunity for all participants to join from the beginning.

Is EMERSON just another meme coin?

While EMERSON embraces the fun and humor of the meme coin culture, we're also focused on building a strong community, ensuring security through contract audits and locked liquidity, and developing real utility for the token over time as outlined in our roadmap.

What blockchain is EMERSON on?

EMERSON is an ERC-20 token built on the Ethereum blockchain, chosen for its security, widespread adoption, and robust ecosystem of tools and services.

Buying Questions

How do I buy EMERSON?

You can purchase EMERSON on decentralized exchanges like Uniswap by connecting your Ethereum wallet and swapping ETH for EMERSON. For detailed instructions, please visit our How To Buy page.

What is the minimum amount I can invest?

There's technically no minimum investment amount for EMERSON. However, you should consider Ethereum gas fees, which can make very small purchases inefficient. We recommend having at least $50-100 worth of ETH to cover both your desired EMERSON purchase and the associated gas fees.

Why do I need to set slippage when buying?

Slippage is the potential difference between the expected price of a trade and the executed price. Because crypto markets can be volatile and EMERSON has a small buy/sell tax, we recommend setting your slippage to 2-3% to ensure your transaction goes through successfully.

Will EMERSON be listed on centralized exchanges?

We're actively working towards listings on centralized exchanges (CEXs) as part of our roadmap. These listings typically come after achieving certain milestones in terms of market cap, holder count, and community size. All exchange listings will be announced through our official channels.

Tokenomics Questions

What is the total supply of EMERSON?

EMERSON has a total supply of 1 trillion (1,000,000,000,000) tokens. This large supply allows for more accessible per-token pricing and permits a wider distribution among holders.

What are the transaction taxes?

EMERSON has a minimal tax structure: 1% on buys and 1% on sells. These taxes are split evenly between marketing (0.5%) and development (0.5%) to ensure the project's continued growth and improvement.

Is there a burn mechanism?

While there isn't an automatic burn mechanism built into the contract, the team may implement strategic burns based on community feedback and market conditions as part of our evolving tokenomics strategy. Any burn events will be announced and transparently executed.

How is the token distribution structured?

The EMERSON token distribution is as follows: 80% for circulating supply available to the public, 10% allocated for marketing efforts, and 10% reserved for development. The marketing and development allocations are subject to a vesting schedule of 1% per month over 10 months to ensure long-term alignment with the project.

Security Questions

Is EMERSON's smart contract audited?

Yes, the EMERSON smart contract has been audited by a reputable security firm to ensure it's free from vulnerabilities and backdoors. The audit report is available for review on our website and social media channels.

Is the liquidity locked?

Yes, initial liquidity has been locked for a period of 1 year to provide security and stability for all EMERSON holders. The liquidity lock can be verified through our contract on Etherscan.

Has the contract ownership been renounced?

Contract ownership will be renounced after the initial setup phase to ensure no centralized control over the token. This step is part of our commitment to creating a truly community-driven project.

How can I verify that EMERSON is legitimate?

You can verify EMERSON's legitimacy by checking our official contract address on Etherscan, reviewing the audit report, confirming the liquidity lock, and joining our official community channels. Always be cautious of scam tokens with similar names and verify you're interacting with the authentic EMERSON token.

Community Questions

How can I join the EMERSON community?

You can join our vibrant community through our Telegram group, Twitter, and Discord channels. Links to all our official communities are available on our website and at the bottom of this page.

How can I contribute to the project?

Are there community events and rewards?

Absolutely! We regularly host community events, contests, and giveaways to reward active members and strengthen our community bonds. These can include meme competitions, trading contests, community challenges, and more. All events are announced in our official channels.
