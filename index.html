FAQ

What is EMERSON?

EMERSON is a community-driven cryptocurrency inspired by a real-life emotional support capuchin monkey named Emerson who helps his owner cope with PTSD. Our project embodies the same supportive spirit in the crypto space.

Is Emerson a real monkey?

Yes! Emerson is a real Brazilian tufted capuchin monkey who serves as an emotional support animal for Lauren Elizabeth McAna from Florida. He helps her cope with PTSD by sensing her emotions and providing comfort during difficult moments.

How does the coin relate to the real Emerson?

Just as the real Emerson provides emotional support to his owner, the EMERSON coin aims to create a supportive community in the often volatile crypto world. We embody the spirit of connection, support, and healing that Emerson represents.

Is EMERSON safe to invest in?

While no investment is without risk, EMERSON has taken steps to ensure safety with locked liquidity, renounced ownership, and a fully audited smart contract.
